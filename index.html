Best quick recipes

Avocado salad

  • 1/2 ripe avocado;
  • Fresh lettuce leaves;
  • cilantro or parsley, dill;
  • Sea salt;
  • Olive oil;
  • Juice of 1⁄4 lemon.

Tear lettuce leaves with your hands. Cut the avocado into cubes. Season the salad with salt, chopped cilantro and olive oil, sprinkle with lemon juice.
